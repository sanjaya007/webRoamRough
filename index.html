About this property

This smoke-free hotel features 15 restaurants, a full-service spa, and a casino. Free WiFi in public areas and free valet parking are also provided. Other amenities include a nightclub, a fitness center, and a sauna. All 671 soundproofed rooms feature free WiFi and iPads. Guests can enjoy deep soaking tubs, and other standard amenities include pillowtop beds, sitting areas, and 24-hour room service.
